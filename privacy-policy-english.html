Privacy Policy - On Call Manager

Last updated: April 2, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy of the On Call Manager application. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

Summary

  • Local storage: All your data is stored locally on your device
  • No external collection: We do not collect or transfer your data to external servers
  • Full control: You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time

Data Collected and Stored

The On Call Manager application collects and stores the following types of information:

  1. Callback data:
    • Dates and times of callbacks
    • Duration of callbacks
    • Reasons for callbacks
    • Hours worked
  2. Financial information:
    • Hourly rates
    • Worked time rates
    • Compensation calculations
  3. User preferences:
    • Application settings
    • Interface preferences
    • Preferred language

Use of Data

The collected data is used only for:

  • Displaying your callbacks and statistics in the application
  • Calculating hours worked and earnings
  • Customizing your user experience

Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device in a local database. We do not use external servers to store or process your data.

Data Sharing

We do not share any of your data with third parties. Your information remains strictly on your device.

Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously:

  • Data is stored in a secure database on your device
  • The application does not have internet access to transfer data
  • You can delete all your data at any time via the reset option in the settings

User Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can access all your data via the application
  • Modification: You can modify your data at any time
  • Deletion: You can delete individual callbacks or reset all data

Parental Consent

Our application is not specifically intended for children under 13 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so that we can take appropriate action.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will inform you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@oncalltracker.app

Consent

By using our application, you consent to our privacy policy.
