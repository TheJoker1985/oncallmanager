FAQ

Is the application free?

Yes, the application is completely free. There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or paid features.

How do I reset my data?

You can reset your data in the application by going to the "Statistics" tab and pressing the "Reset on-call sequence" button.

Is the application available in multiple languages?

Yes, the application is available in French and English.

Is the application available for iPad?

No, the application is optimized only for iPhone and Android devices. It is not available for iPad.
